Robotics 5

The 3rd and final course in the Elementary school robotics program.

Syllabus Supplies
Grades: 5
Length: 8 Sessions
Teacher: Mr. Cooper

Robotics 5 is the final course in the elementary robotics sequence, it allows students to gain a deeper undstanding of logic and programming using robitcs. Spike Prime kits are used for fast builds and enable students to rapidly prototype ideas.

  • Meeting 1: Connecting the Robot & Rock Paper Scissors Robot
  • Meeting 2: Programming the Hub
  • Meeting 3: Motors & Wind Turbine Design
  • Meeting 4: Distance Sensor & Goalie
