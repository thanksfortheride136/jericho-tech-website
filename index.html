Jericho Technology Program

At Jericho UFSD, students explore engineering, computer science, and design through hands-on projects. From laser-cut instruments and 3D-printed prototypes to coding, electronics, and iterative product design, our courses emphasize creativity, problem-solving, and real-world technical skills in an inclusive, high-energy lab environment.
