Robotics 3

Design process, rapid prototyping, CAD fundamentals, laser cutting, and 3D printing.

Syllabus Supplies
Grades: 7–8
Length: Semester
Prerequisite: None
Teacher: Mr. Cooper

In Robotics 3 students learn the engineering design process through quick-build mechanisms and rapid prototyping. We cover CAD basics, laser safety, 3D printing workflows, and a short capstone challenge.

  • Unit 1: Design Process & Sketching
  • Unit 2: CAD + Laser Cutting
  • Unit 3: 3D Printing & Iteration
