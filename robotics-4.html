Robotics 4

The 2nd course in the Elementary school robotics program.

Syllabus Supplies
Grades: 4
Length: 4 Sessions
Teacher: Mr. Cooper

Robotics 4 builds upon the foundational skills students developed in 3rd grade. This year the course focuses on deepening students’ understanding that computers and robots are only as smart as their programmer. Students will explore the role of sensors and their importance in robotics by designing and programming a simple car, which will gradually become more complex as the course progresses.

  • Meeting 1: Connecting the Robot & Creator Logic
  • Meeting 2: Constructing the "Cantiague Rover"
  • Meeting 3: Distance Sensors
  • Meeting 4: Sensor Party: Distance, Push Button, Color Sensor
